Activities during Montecito Family Camp

Montecito is at its absolute best during our Summer Family Camp! During Family Camp we offer far more activities than at any other time of the year. Plus, our staff will supervise your children, from ages 2 - 18, in our Family Camp Children's Program, for almost 6-hours each camp day. This allows the grown ups to enjoy their own activities, knowing that their children are well cared for. Our families come together during mealtimes, and during our family-oriented evening programs.

Here's a taste of what we offer. Please be aware, however, that each year some of our activities change due to weather-conditions, staff expertise, or guest preferences, but, rest assured, you'll NEVER be bored at Montecito Family Camp! We strive every summer to be better than the previous one.

You won't have time to do everything at Montecito, our offerings are too vast...that's why our guests return year-after-year to try new things, and indulge in their old favorites. Make sure to check out our Sample Daily Family Camp Schedule to see what a typical day at camp looks like!
