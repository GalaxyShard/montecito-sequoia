If you want your family to experience the BEST that Montecito has to offer, then join us for one of our Summer Family Camp Weeks. Renowned throughout the United States, Montecito Sequoia's Summer Family Camp guarantees fun for the whole family, and will create memories that will last a lifetime!

Parents, grandparents, young children to teens, we offer a range of fun activities for everyone. Come find out why many of our families return year-after-year, and invite more friends and family to join in the fun with them. In fact, we have many large family celebrations and reunions during Family Camp, because it is perfect for all ages!

Our All-inclusive packages include meals, lodging, and full-array of activities for everyone in your family, with almost 6 hours of quality child-care for your children, ages 2 - 18, daily! Children under the age of 2 are free!

Learn about our All-Inclusive Dining

Our Family Camp Weeks begin in June and run through August, consisting of ten 6-night sessions that you can choose from. We offer a variety of Activities each day for all ages and skill levels, including our popular Children's Program. These include land sports, water activities, and a horse program. Each evening we provide a Special Event for the whole family to enjoy together.

There are optional group programs for children ages 2 - 18. Each age group has its own animal mascot and is led by an experienced counselor. Kids can have fun and make new friends, while parents and grandparents can enjoy the many adult activities on offer. No one is required to join the group programs. Remember, all activities are free choice! Learn more about our Children's Program.

Family campers and staff share the same meals and social hours, creating a sense of community. Home-cooked, buffet-style meals are wholesome, nutritious, and California fresh, with vegetarian options, a salad bar, and fresh fruit. Planning a hike in the Sierras? We'll have the fixins for a trail lunch ready for you in the morning. We also offer a 24-hour complimentary snack bar, stocked with coffee, tea, juice, fresh fruit, cereal, bagels, and various other snacks, so you'll never go hungry at Montecito!

Montecito offers a variety of All-Inclusive Rates & Accommodations for your family's comfort and price point. From Vintage Lodge Rooms & Suites, with private baths, to Value-priced Rustic Cabins with a shared bathhouse, to our New Forest Cabins and upscale Lakeview Suites, both with private baths & decks, you'll be sure to find the one that is right for your family.

Since our rates are "all-inclusive," they may appear expensive to some at first glance, but our guests tell us that they appreciate the excellent value they receive during Family Camp, especially since meals, child care, activities, and lots of fun are already included. Plus we offer many discounted weeks, a referral discount, and an early payment discount, to help our families lower their overall Family Camp rates.

Days at Montecito Family Camp are warm and sunny; nights are cool, clear, and studded with stars. Montecito Sequoia Lodge is surrounded by spectacular mountains, lush green meadows, and miles of trails and streams. Life here is designed to be carefree. Come join the Montecito Family and start a new tradition of Family Camp inside the gates of Sequoia & Kings Canyon National Parks.
