2023 Weekly Themes for Montecito Family Camp

2023

PIRATE PARRRRRTY!
Week 1 (June 11 - 17)
Ahoy Mateys! Shiver me timbers...there are Pirates invading the shores of Lake Homavalo! Prepare to fend off the ne're-do-wells with water balloons and cannon- balls. Then we'll all celebrate by dancing the hornpipe in our best pirate costume! ARRGGHHH!!!!

CIRCUS, CIRCUS!!
Week 2 (June 18 - 24)
Popcorn...Peanuts...Cotton Candy here!! Ladies and gentlemen, and children of all ages...come join the Montecito Circus and be part of the Greatest Show on Earth!!! In our Big Top under the stars, our performers will delight and amaze you! Come dressed for an evening of fun!

THROUGH THE DECADES
Week 3 (June 25 - July 1)
Travel back in time to your favorite decade! Come as a flapper, gangster, greaser, bobbysoxer, hippie, or disco dancer. So many decades, so many awesome costume choices...show us your favorite vintage costume finds!

HELLO HOLLYWOOD / JULY 4th CELEBRATION
Week 4 (July 2 - July 8)
Join us on the Red Carpet as your favorite movie star or celebrity -- but watch out for the paparazzi! Of course, celebrities aren't the only stars you'll see here at Montecito. After stargazing at our Oscar soiree, we'll be celebrating the Stars & Stripes, with our annual Bark Boat Regatta by the lake a Montecito July 4th tradition!

ON BROADWAY!
Week 5 (July 9 - 15)
Our Montecito cast and crew are Broadway bound for one night only and you are up for the lead!!! What role will you play? From "Alexander Hamilton" to "Phantom of the Opera," "Annie" to "Westside Story" -- you are the star of our show! "Tonight, tonight..."

HARRY POTTER PARTY!
Week 6 (July 16 - 22)
Montecito will transform into Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry for one night, while you magically transform into your favorite Harry Potter character! Dance the night away with Harry, Hermione, and Ron, while we all protect the muggles from evil Lord Voldemort! Come cast your spell for a magical evening!

MAD HATTER PARTY!
Week 7 (July 23 - 29)
You've been invited to tea by the Mad Hatter himself! The Dress Code: Wear your most fun and imaginative bonnet, fascinator, or chapeau, OR follow Alice down the rabbit hole and dress as your favorite 'Wonderland' character! The Queen of Hearts will be our special guest, so don't be late for this very important (dance) date!

FAMILY GAME NIGHT
Week 8 (July 30 - Aug 5)
AGES: 2 & Up; RATING: "E" (Everyone); PLAYERS: 1-200
From "Candyland" to "Candy CRUSH," Monopoly" to "Minecraft," everybody wins when you show us your best moves at our Montecito "Family Game Night" dance! Everyone's a winner at Montecito!

DISNEY PARTY
Week 9 (Aug 6 - 12)
Come celebrate the "House of Mouse" at Montecito! Whether you are a Disney Princess or Goofy at heart, now's your chance to share your love of all things Disney! From Mickey and Minnie to Elsa and Buzz Lightyear, you are sure to see all your Disney favorites!

SCI-FI/FANTASY PARTY
Week 10 (Aug 13 - 19)
In a galaxy far, far away...there was a dance party that traveled through space and time to Montecito! Get ready for an intergalactic adventure filled with your favorite space heroes and villains. Join us and dance at warp. speed under the canopy of our own Milky Way Galaxy!

Do not forget to bring your costumes, musical instruments, etc., to fully participate in all our special events
*Welcome Reception *Open-mic Coffee House Night (songs, music, poems!) *Nightly sing-along campfires *Variety Show (skits, dance, magic!) *Beach Party and more!

Bring a sleeping bag for kids ages 7 & Up for Overnights!
