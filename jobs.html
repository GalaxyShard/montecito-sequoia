Year-round, seasonal winter, and summer seasonal jobs

We offer hospitality positions year-round, but also offer many seasonal jobs during our summer high-season, including approximately 30 camp counselor positions, for our renowned Summer Family Camp (We will begin accepting applications for summer positions on Jan. 1st). We also offer winter seasonal jobs (especially for snow sports & activities counselors).

We welcome employees from the US and around the world
We welcome both local and US Nationals who love the great outdoors, and also encourage international job candidates who have a strong command of English to apply. Montecito Sequoia has a long history of employing international counselors, particularly for our summer family camp counselor positions. We even host an International Night as part of our Summer Family Camp program, as our camp families love to meet and experience counselors from around the world.
